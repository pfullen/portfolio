About Peter


I have over 20 years experience in Accounting , Tax and Consulting. I also assists businesses in setting up cloud based business applications. I am experienced in mySQL and MSSQL databases. My development experience includes PHP and JAVA. Over the past year (2014) I have also added HTML5, CSS3 and jQuery to my tool belt. Thanks to the ThinkFul Front-End course and working closely with a mentor each week I was able to master these skills.

I have a unique ability to understand both accounting/tax and highly technical issues. By understanding the business process and work flow I can assist clients with converting time consuming manual tasks to online forms and reports that can be connected to their QuickBooks or ERP/accounting system.

My hobbies include programming, cooking, cycling, running, camping, astronomy and learning French.
